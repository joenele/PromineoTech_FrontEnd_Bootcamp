About

Mobile Resellers is a user-to-user marketplace for buying and selling new and gently used technology.
Buyers and sellers connect directly and all devices must be fully functional to be sold or listed on Mobile Resellers.
Mobile Resellers employs a customer service team that reviews listed devices to ensure they meet this requirement.

Mobile Resellers was founded by Joenele Young and launched on February 1st, 2023.
Mobile Resellers is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, and is a remote workplace with employees spread
across the United States and around the world including Ecuador, Greece, India, and Thailand.

The Mobile Resellers marketplace was launched with the goal of building a safer and simpler marketplace.
It was launched to serve as an alternative to other online marketplaces such as eBay and Craigslist.
